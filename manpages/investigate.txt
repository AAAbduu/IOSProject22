NAME: 
        investigate  -  carry out research and study into an object

SYNOPSIS:
        investigate [item] 

DESCRIPTION: 
        Make investigations on objects which must be interesting for our problem and which must exist,
        the use of this command sometimes leads to the removal of certain restrictions other times to 
        simple reflections or doubts on some thing or someone
        

SYSTEM CALLS:
        open, read, close, readdir

C LIBRARY FUNCTIONS:
        opendir, closedir

RETURN VALUE:
        'There is no such object to investigate...'
        or
        investigation will be made
