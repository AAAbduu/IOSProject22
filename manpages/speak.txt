NAME: 
        speak  -  talk to a character

SYNOPSIS:
        speak
        speak [character]

DESCRIPTION: 
        Speak shows the characters pissible to chat with in a given place, or the words exchanged with a character if followed by his name.

SYSTEM CALLS:
        open, read, write, close, readdir, lseek

C LIBRARY FUNCTIONS:
        opendir, closedir

RETURN VALUE:
        Scenario swapped
